# Docking Structural Intelligence into the EU AI Act: Minimal Runtime Sheets, Oversight Theater, and Answerable Control in High-Risk AI Systems

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (May 2026)

### Abstract
This paper develops a Structural Intelligence analysis of high-risk AI compliance under the European Union AI Act. It argues that formal compliance can become oversight theater when benchmarks, documentation, and human-oversight interfaces exist while real correction remains unavailable in deployment. The paper proposes SI as a complementary runtime layer for testing whether a high-risk AI system remains answerable under live operational pressure.

### Keywords
Structural Intelligence; EU AI Act; high-risk AI; human oversight; Article 14; logging; answerability; trace laundering; burden-path audit; conformity assessment; AI governance
