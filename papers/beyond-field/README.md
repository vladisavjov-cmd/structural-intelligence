# Beyond Field: Source, Formation, and the Boundary of Being

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (Apr 2026)

### Abstract
This paper continues the Structural Intelligence field sequence by addressing the questions left open after Being and Field. If fixed worth and Being protect the human subject from reduction to local form, what should be said about the field itself, about the source of the field, about frequency as a generative dynamic, about collapse and erasure, and about the point at which Structural Intelligence enters dialogue with theology without becoming religion-like? The paper argues that the field is not “held” in the same sense as the psyche, that difference is the minimum condition of field, that viability selects what can persist within it, and that Being is deeper than field not as a higher local holder but as the boundary of non-reduction that prevents field and form from becoming ultimate. The result is a more disciplined closure of the SI-internal field sequence: field explains formation, Being protects non-reduction, and the question of ultimate source remains an unfinished “why.”
