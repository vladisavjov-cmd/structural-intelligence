# Structural Dynamics of Structuration: Presence, Occupancy, Debt, Boundary, and the Stabilization of Relation

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Journal article  
**Version:** Latest archived (Apr 2026)

### Abstract
This paper develops a structural account of how relation is stabilized through presence, occupancy, debt, and boundary. It examines how local structurations hold a field together, how steering can become occupied, how debt accumulates when contact is deferred, and how boundaries regulate the viability of relation across scales. The result is a dynamic account of structuration that treats stabilization not as static order but as ongoing answerable organization under pressure.

### Keywords
structuration; presence; occupancy; debt; boundary; stabilization; relation; local structuration; answerability; structural intelligence
