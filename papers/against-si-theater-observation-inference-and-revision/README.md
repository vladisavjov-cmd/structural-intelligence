# Against SI-Theater: Observation, Inference, and Revision in Structural Analysis

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (May 2026)

### Abstract
This paper develops a methodological self-critique of Structural Intelligence. It argues that SI becomes weaker when its own vocabulary is allowed to manufacture hidden depth too quickly. A valid SI reading must separate observation from inference, show uncertainty, allow “not enough evidence,” and remain revisable by consequence, missing information, and falsifiers.

### Keywords
Structural Intelligence; SI-theater; observation; inference; revision; falsifiers; epistemic integrity; AI discourse; ontology drift; public method
