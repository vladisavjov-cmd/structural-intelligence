# Structural Intelligence: Coherence, Contact, and Answerability Under Pressure

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Book  
**Version:** Latest archived (Apr 2026)

### Abstract
This book presents Structural Intelligence as a framework for distinguishing what truly holds under reality from what only performs holding through local coherence. Its central grammar is coherence, contact, and answerability. From that grammar, the book develops a general account of holding, failure, burden, occupancy, collapse, human–AI asymmetry, and the conditions under which revision remains possible before consequence returns in a harder form. Structural Intelligence asks whether a person, relationship, institution, argument, or AI system remains coherent, contactful, and answerable under pressure, or whether it preserves appearance by thinning contact, exporting burden, or avoiding revision.
