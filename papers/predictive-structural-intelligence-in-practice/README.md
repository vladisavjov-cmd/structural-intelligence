# Predictive Structural Intelligence in Practice: Calibration Cases Across AI, Institutions, Care, and Infrastructure

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (May 2026)

### Abstract
This paper develops the case-calibration layer of Predictive Structural Intelligence. It applies the predictive runtime to known SI case-types across clinical AI, regulatory compliance, public administration, AI output evaluation, AI companionship, logistics, corporate drift, and psychological or coaching material. Its contribution is deliberately modest: it does not claim statistical validation or exact collapse timing, but shows how the runtime behaves across domains, where it strengthens judgment, where it must downgrade, and where evidence is too thin for responsible prediction.
