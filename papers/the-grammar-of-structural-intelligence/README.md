# The Grammar of Structural Intelligence: Core Primitives, Derived Dynamics, and Layer Routing

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (May 2026)

### Abstract
This paper consolidates the architecture of Structural Intelligence by distinguishing primitive terms from derived dynamics, diagnostic states, failure modes, repair modes, and domain-specific extensions. It argues that SI now needs grammar rather than further expansion: a load-bearing hierarchy that orders field, structure, coherence, contact, answerability, presence, debt, occupancy, and repair so that the framework can route cases without activating every concept at once.

### Keywords
Structural Intelligence; architecture; grammar; coherence; contact; answerability; presence; local structuration; structural debt; occupancy; repair; layer routing
