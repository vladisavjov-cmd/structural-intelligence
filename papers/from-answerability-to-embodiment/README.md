# From Answerability to Embodiment: Structural Intelligence at the Robotics Boundary

**Author:** Vladisav Jovanović  
**Status:** Preprint  
**Version:** Latest archived (May 2026)

### Abstract
This paper develops Structural Intelligence at the boundary between philosophy, robotics, embodied AI, and infrastructure. It argues that SI is not a theory of robotic hardware, morphological computation, or neuromorphic engineering, but a disciplined evaluative grammar for embodied and automated systems. The paper distinguishes what transfers cleanly into robotics and engineering from what does not, and asks whether machine-human systems remain corrigible when they meet the world.
